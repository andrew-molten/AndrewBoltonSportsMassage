Sports Massage

Beneficial for athletes, active people or anybody with tension. Regular sports massage can help increase flexibility, realign muscle fibres, and restore muscle length, shape and strength. It can also be used to aid training to reduce fatigue, injury and delayed onset muscle soreness, allowing you to perform at your best.

Relaxing massage

Whole body therapeutic massage designed to relax your muscles and joints, stimulate blood flow and calm your nervous system. Long rhythmic strokes with pressure that is gentle, moderate or deep to your preference. Unwind to peaceful music and relaxing scents.
