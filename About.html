About

Sports Massage

I am a kiwi that has lived in around the world and found my way back to NZ. I am a strong, profressional & friendly massage therapist, I graduated as a sports massage therapist in the UK in early 2019 and have been practicing ever since.

Treatments available to ease tight muscles, to help heal old injuries, to compliment your training routine or just to relax. I tailor each massage to the individual for what they need and whether you like deep tissue, or prefer a lighter touch. I practice out of my home studio that I keep nice and warm (nothing worse than a cold massage). As part of the consultation, I also suggest strengthening/flexiblility exercises to optimize your bodys performance, recovery and healing.

Yoga

I first started practicing yoga in 2010 when old lifting habits and sporting injuries lead me on a journey to heal my body. It didn’t take long to get hooked on the good feelings of the practice and through yoga and massage I recovered more strength and mobility and became super enthused on the subject. I trained with Tony Sanchez in Mexico to learn in-depth about the Ghosh lineage practice of hatha yoga in 2014, and then dove deeper into the vinyasa practice with a 200 hour certificate in 2016.

I like to teach a full range of styles including: gentle, strong, beginners, flowy, static and relaxing yin yoga (which is probably what I am most passionate about and what most active people could really use in their life. I’m now offering private yoga sessions - get in touch if you would like to book one. Please also let me know if you’re interested in regular classes.
