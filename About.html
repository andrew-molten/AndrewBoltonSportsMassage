About

Sports Massage

I am a kiwi that has lived around the world and found my way back to NZ. I am a strong, professional & friendly massage therapist, graduating as a sports massage therapist in the UK in early 2019 and have been practicing ever since.

Treatments available to ease tight muscles, help heal old injuries, compliment your training routine or just to help you relax.

I tailor each massage to the individual, for whatever you need, while also taking into account whether you like deep tissue or prefer a lighter touch.

I practice out of my cosy home studio that I keep nice and warm. As part of the consultation, I also suggest strengthening/flexiblility exercises to optimize your bodys performance, recovery and healing.
