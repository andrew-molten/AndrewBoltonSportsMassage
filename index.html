Welcome

My focus is sports massage, deep tissue massage and yoga exercises, so it is my goal to not only relieve your pain and tension, but also assist in figuring out the cause. Whether associated with injury, imbalance, dysfunction or disease. Through a constantly growing knowledge of how our bodies work and move I use various techniques to release soft tissue and break down adhesions, relieving pain and muscle tension so you can move more freely, more efficiently and relax in your own body.

Benefits of Massage Therapy include:
Pain & tension relief
Injury prevention
Reduced recovery time of an injury or surgery
Stress relief
Improved flexibility
Help relieve headaches
Help reduce depression and anxiety
