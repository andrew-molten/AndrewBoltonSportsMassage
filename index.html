Welcome

Here at Andrew Bolton Sports Massage it's my goal to relieve your pain and tension, so that you can feel relaxed and rejuvenated.

My specialty is deep tissue massage, but I also offer a range of other massage techniques including ralaxation, sports, and prenatal. We use a combination of techniques to help you achieve your desired results, whether that be to reduce pain, alleviate tension or simply unwind and relax.

I am passionate about helping people achieve a life free from pain. I firmly believe that cultivating balance accross various aspects of our physical and mental well-being help in this quest, including strength, flexibility, breathing techniques, mobility, nutrition, meditation & cardiovascular health.
