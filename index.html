Welcome
to Andrew Bolton Sports Massage!

My goal is to relieve your pain & tension, so that you can feel relaxed, pain free & live your best life!

My specialty is deep tissue massage, but I also offer a range of other massage styles including relaxation, sports & prenatal. I use a combination of techniques to help you achieve results, whether that's to overcome injury, reduce pain, alleviate tension or simply unwind & relax.

Through constantly growing knowledge of how our bodies work & move, I use various techniques to release soft tissue & break down adhesions, relieving pain & muscle tension so you can move more freely, more efficiently & relax in your own body.

I am passionate about helping people achieve a life free from pain. I firmly believe that cultivating balance accross various aspects of our physical & mental well-being help in this quest, including strength, flexibility, breathing, mobility, nutrition, meditation, cardio health & more!

My primary objective is to quickly get you to a place where you don't need me anymore because you feel great in your body! But of course if you love regular maintenance massages, then you're always welcome!

I tailor each massage to the individual, for whatever you need, while also taking into account whether you like deep tissue or prefer a lighter touch.
