Welcome

I focus on sports massage, deep tissue massage & yoga exercises.

My goal is to relieve your pain and tension through massage while also helping to figuring out the root cause.

I also suggest tools & exercises relevant to your situation to help overcome your injury or prevent pains injuries from coming back!

Through a constantly growing knowledge of how our bodies work and move I use various techniques to release soft tissue and break down adhesions, relieving pain and muscle tension so you can move more freely, more efficiently and relax in your own body.

Benefits of Massage Therapy include:
Pain & tension relief
Injury prevention
Reduced recovery time from an injury or surgery
Stress relief
Improved flexibility
Headache relief
Can help reduce depression and anxiety
